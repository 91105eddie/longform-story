# Review Checklist

## Story Core

- The story can be summarized in one clear sentence.
- The protagonist's external goal is clear.
- The protagonist's internal need is clear.
- The theme appears through choices and consequences, not only explanation.
- The conflict cannot be solved by a simple conversation or obvious policy fix.

## Worldbuilding

- The AI governance system has rules and limits.
- The power structure benefits specific groups.
- The public has believable reasons to accept the system.
- Daily life can produce scenes, not only exposition.
- Technology creates tradeoffs, not magic solutions.

## Character

- Every major character wants something specific.
- Every major character has a fear, flaw, or contradiction.
- Relationships create pressure on decisions.
- The protagonist's flaw connects to the world theme.
- Supporting characters test different answers to freedom versus safety.

## Plot

- Each episode or chapter changes the situation.
- Causality is clear: events happen because of prior choices.
- The ending of each unit creates a new problem or irreversible change.
- Foreshadowing is tracked.
- Timeline and character ages are coherent.

## Animation Readiness

- Scenes are visible and performable.
- Internal thoughts are converted into action, dialogue, imagery, voiceover, or sound.
- Key locations are defined.
- Key props and technology are defined.
- The script can be broken into shots.

## Production Readiness

- Deliverables are named.
- Dependencies are visible.
- Blockers are recorded.
- Asset needs are listed.
- The next concrete task is always clear.
